## -DAn daha + sifat karsilastirma (than in Turkish)
---

When comparing two things the structure `X is comparative adj. + than Y` in Turkish has a pattern `X Y-DAn daha + adj.`. 

🗒️ Note

    - In Turkish, the comparator comes after the things being compared as it is suffix based.
    Example would be "Summer is **better than** Winter(Yaz kış**tan daha iyidir**)."

Let us now break the suffix **-DAn daha** where

- **D** → d or t
- **A** → a or e
<span className="text-blue-500 font-semibold"></span>

| Last Vowel  | Last Letter | than |
| --- | --- | --- |
| <span className="text-blue-500 font-semibold">a-ı-o-u</span> | one of [ <span className="text-cyan-500 font-semibold">f, s, t, k, ç, ş, h, p</span> ] | -**tan daha** |
| <span className="text-blue-500 font-semibold">a-ı-o-u</span> | one of [ <span className="text-cyan-500 font-semibold">other letters</span> ] | -**dan daha** |
| <span className="text-blue-500 font-semibold">e-i-ö-ü</span> | one of [ <span className="text-cyan-500 font-semibold">f, s, t, k, ç, ş, h, p</span> ] | -**ten daha** |
| <span className="text-blue-500 font-semibold">e-i-ö-ü</span> | one of [ <span className="text-cyan-500 font-semibold">other letters</span> ] | -**den daha** |
