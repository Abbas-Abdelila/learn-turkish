## The most… en + sıfat in Turkish
---

In this article, we take things to the extreme. We will learn about how to talk about that are on the extreme edge of any spectrum. The most, the best, the greatest, the fastest, the slowest…. you get it.

The grammar structure is very simple.

- en + adjective

It is used to convey superlatives, similar to how "the most" or "the -est" is used in English.

Examples

| English  | Turkish |
| --- | --- |
| The best | En iyi |
| The worst | En kötü |
| The highest | En yüksek |
| The lowest | En düşük |
| The most valuable | En değerli |
| The most satisfying | En tatmin edici |
| The most important | En önemli |

Let us see this in action.

 This is the best movie I've ever seen.

- Bu, şimdiye kadar gördüğüm en iyi film.

That was the worst meal I've ever had.

- Bu, yediğim en kötü yemekti.

Mount Everest is the highest peak in the world.

- Everest Dağı dünyanın en yüksek zirvesidir.

This is the lowest temperature we've experienced this winter.

- Bu, bu kış yaşadığımız en düşük sıcaklık.

The most valuable thing in life is good health.

- Hayatta en değerli şey iyi sağlıktır.

Completing a challenging project is the most satisfying feeling.

- Zorlu bir proje tamamlamak en tatmin edici his.

The most important lesson I've learned is to be kind to others.

- Öğrendiğim en önemli ders başkalarına nazik olmaktır.
