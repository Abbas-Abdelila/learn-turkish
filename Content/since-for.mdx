## Since… -DAn beri and For.. DIr in Turkish
---

I am going to explain to you how you can say or write sentences like “I play football since high school or I live in San Francisco since 2019”. 

We will also learn for how long we have been doing something in Turkish. 

Let us start by learning few things about “Since”. (since it is important 😎)

Turkish is all about suffix. The suffix for `(ever) since` is -**DAn** <span className="text-red-500 font-medium">beri</span>, where

- **D** → **d** or **t**
- **A** → **a** or **e**

It expresses the duration of time that passed after a specific event or point in time.

This suffix is used to explain the <span className="text-blue-500">time related</span> **since** but not the <span className="text-blue-500">reason related</span> in English. There is another suffix for that which we will learn later. 

| Last Vowel  | Last Letter | Since |
| --- | --- | --- |
| <span className="text-blue-500">a-ı-o-u</span> | one of [ <span className="text-cyan-500">f, s, t, k, ç, ş, h, p</span> ] | -**tan** <span className="text-red-500 font-medium">beri</span> |
| <span className="text-blue-500">a-ı-o-u</span> | one of [ <span className="text-cyan-500">other letters</span> ] | -**dan** <span className="text-red-500 font-medium">beri</span> |
| <span className="text-blue-500">e-i-ö-ü</span> | one of [ <span className="text-cyan-500">f, s, t, k, ç, ş, h, p</span> ] | -**ten** <span className="text-red-500 font-medium">beri</span> |
| <span className="text-blue-500">e-i-ö-ü</span> | one of [ <span className="text-cyan-500">other letters</span> ] | -**den** <span className="text-red-500 font-medium">beri</span> |

#### Examples

Üç ay**dan** <span className="text-red-500 font-medium">beri</span> Türkçe öğreniyorum.

    - I am learning Turkish **since** three months.


Amerika’da iki yıl**dan** <span className="text-red-500 font-medium">beri</span> yaşıyorum.

    - I am living in America **since** two years.


Dört yaşım**dan** <span className="text-red-500 font-medium">beri</span> okula gidiyorum.

    - I go to school **since** I was four years old.


Yağmur sabah**tan** <span className="text-red-500 font-medium">beri</span> yağıyorç

    - It is raining **since** morning.


Covid geldik**ten** <span className="text-red-500 font-medium">beri</span> dünya çok değişti.

    - The world changed drastically **since** Covid.


Naval Ravikant’a rastladık**tan** <span className="text-red-500 font-medium">beri</span> düşüncelerim değişti.

    - My thinking has changed **ever since** I came across Naval Ravikant.  

---


Now let us see `For` as in I have been waiting for this for years.

The suffix is -**DIr** where 

- D → **d** or **t**
- I →  **i**, **ı**, **u**, or **ü**

They are added to adverbs of time like

- <span className="text-green-400 font-semibold">hafta</span><span className="text-blue-900 font-semibold">lar</span>**dır** (**for** <span className="text-green-400 font-semibold">week</span><span className="text-blue-900 font-semibold">s</span>)
- <span className="text-green-400 font-semibold">ay</span><span className="text-blue-900 font-semibold">lar</span>**dır** (**for** <span className="text-green-400 font-semibold">month</span><span className="text-blue-900 font-semibold">s</span>)
- <span className="text-green-400 font-semibold">yıl</span><span className="text-blue-900 font-semibold">lar</span>**dır** (**for** <span className="text-green-400 font-semibold">year</span><span className="text-blue-900 font-semibold">s</span>)
- <span className="text-blue-900 font-semibold">X</span> <span className="text-green-400 font-semibold">saat</span>**tir**[^1] (**for** <span className="text-blue-900 font-semibold">X</span> <span className="text-green-400 font-semibold">hour</span><span className="text-blue-900 font-semibold">(s)</span>)
- <span className="text-blue-900 font-semibold">Y</span> <span className="text-green-400 font-semibold">dakika</span>**dır** (**for** <span className="text-blue-900 font-semibold">Y</span> <span className="text-green-400 font-semibold">minute</span><span className="text-blue-900 font-semibold">(s)</span>)

| Last Vowel | Last Letter | For … |
| --- | --- | --- |
| <span className="text-blue-500 font-semibold">a-ı</span> | one of [ <span className="text-cyan-500">f, s, t, k, ç, ş, h, p</span> ] | -**tır** |
| <span className="text-blue-500 font-semibold">a-ı</span> | one of [ <span className="text-cyan-500">other letters</span> ] | -**dır** |
| <span className="text-blue-500 font-semibold">o-u</span> | one of [ <span className="text-cyan-500">f, s, t, k, ç, ş, h, p</span> ] | -**tur** |
| <span className="text-blue-500 font-semibold">o-u</span> | one of [ <span className="text-cyan-500">other letters</span> ] | -**dur** |
| <span className="text-blue-500 font-semibold">e-i</span> | one of [ <span className="text-cyan-500">f, s, t, k, ç, ş, h, p</span> ] | -**tir** |
| <span className="text-blue-500 font-semibold">e-i</span> | one of [ <span className="text-cyan-500">other letters</span> ] | -**dir** |
| <span className="text-blue-500 font-semibold">ö-ü</span> | one of [ <span className="text-cyan-500">f, s, t, k, ç, ş, h, p</span> ] | -**tür** |
| <span className="text-blue-500 font-semibold">ö-ü</span> | one of [ <span className="text-cyan-500">other letters</span> ] | -**dür** |

---

#### Examples

Kopek iki gün**dür** kayıp.

    - The dog is missing **for** two days.

Arkadaşım birkaç hafta**dır** tatil planı yapıyor.

    - My friend is making holiday plans **for** few weeks.

Müzik grubu konser için birkaç ay**dır** prova yapıyor.

    - The music band is rehearsing **for** few months for the concert.

Gül festivalı iki hafta**dır** devam ediyor.

    - The rose festival is going on **for** two weeks.

Çocuklar oyuncağını beş saat**tir** arıyor.

    - The kids are looking **for** their toy for five hours.
    


---

[^1]: The word `Saat` is one of the exceptions of our general formula where the suffix is -t**i**r instead of -t**ı**r.It is because the word `saat` is a foreign word adopted from Arabic.
